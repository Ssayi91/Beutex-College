How to Apply

You don’t need to be confident yet.

Many students arrive unsure — about their ability, their future, or even where to begin.

Admissions is not a test. It’s a conversation.

How Admissions Works

1

Choose a Course

Review our courses and think about what interests you. If unsure, we help you decide honestly.

2

Talk to Us

Call or WhatsApp us. We explain the course, duration, fees, and expectations clearly.

3

Visit or Register

You may visit the campus or register remotely. We guide you through the process.

4

Start Learning

Once registered, you join classes and begin hands-on training immediately.

Entry Requirements

Our courses are open to beginners. You do not need previous experience in beauty or computers.

  • ✔ Willingness to learn
  • ✔ Commitment to attend classes
  • ✔ Basic reading and communication skills

If you are unsure whether you qualify, talk to us first. We assess individually.

Fees & Flexibility

Affordable Fees

Our fees are structured to remain accessible. Details depend on the course selected.

Flexible Schedules

We understand different responsibilities. Scheduling options may be available.

Clear Communication

No hidden costs. Everything is explained before you commit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No. Our programs are designed for beginners. Training starts from the basics.

Can I visit before registering?

Yes. Visiting helps you understand the environment and ask questions.

How long are the courses?

Duration depends on the course. Full details are provided during inquiry.

Is certification provided?

Yes. Students receive certification upon completion.

Ready to Take the First Step?

If you’re still unsure, that’s okay. The first step is simply talking to us.
